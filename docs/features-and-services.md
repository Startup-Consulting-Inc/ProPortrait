# ProPortrait AI — Features & Services

> A non-technical overview of what ProPortrait AI does and what it offers to users.

---

## What It Does

**ProPortrait AI** is an AI-powered professional portrait generator. You upload a photo and get studio-quality professional headshots in seconds. The AI keeps your identity consistent — eye color, skin tone, and hair — so the result looks like you, not a generic face. Portraits are ready for professional use (LinkedIn, resumes, team pages, and more).

## Services & Features

### 1. Personalized Onboarding

New users complete a short 2-step flow so the app can tailor portrait settings:

- **Purpose & industry** — e.g. job search, executive, creative, services, arts, and your field.
- **Vibe & use cases** — e.g. polished, warm, bold, or creative, and where you’ll use the photo.

From this, the app suggests:

- **Style** — e.g. editorial for corporate, watercolor for creative.
- **Expression** — e.g. confident for executives, warm smile for client-facing roles.
- **Identity locks** — e.g. glasses on for legal, more natural for artists.
- **Skin smoothing** — natural vs. polished vs. studio.
- **Background preferences** — e.g. dark for executives, warm neutrals for services.

You can retake the onboarding anytime from your profile.

---

### 2. Upload

- Upload a photo (JPG, PNG, or WEBP; max 10 MB).
- Drag-and-drop or click to browse.
- A short privacy notice explains how your photo is used.
- Tips show what makes a good source photo and what to avoid.

---

### 3. Style & Settings

**Seven portrait styles:**

| Style | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Editorial | High-fashion editorial look with bold lighting |
| Environmental | Natural setting, environmental portrait |
| Candid | Warm, natural candid look |
| Vintage | 1950s–60s Kodachrome film look |
| B&W | Fine-art black and white |
| Cyberpunk | Neon-lit futuristic portrait |
| Watercolor | Soft painterly watercolor style |

**Controls you can adjust:**

- **Group photos** — Choose which person to use: single person, left, center, right, or describe in your own words.
- **Expression** — Presets: Confident, Warm Smile, Serious, Natural.
- **Identity locks** — Toggles for eye color, skin tone, hair length (on by default); hair texture and glasses (off by default).
- **Likeness strength** — How close to your photo (0–100; default 75).
- **Skin smoothness / naturalness** — With presets: Natural, Polished, Studio (Studio is for paid use).
- **Blemish removal** — On by default.
- **Variations** — Generate 2 or 4 images per run.
- **Identity Confidence Score** — A simple meter showing how well your identity is preserved with current settings.
- **Copy settings** — Copy your current settings for sharing or reusing.

**Two modes:**

- **Quick mode (default)** — Curated styles, one-click generate, “Edit Portrait” opens the editor when you need it, simple export.
- **Advanced mode** — All 7 styles, full controls (expressions, identity locks, sliders, group photo, variations), editor always visible, full export options.

---

### 4. Editing Studio

- **Variation thumbnails** — Switch between the generated images with one click.
- **Before/after slider** — Drag to compare your original photo and the AI portrait.
- **AI editor** (via “Edit Portrait” in Quick mode, or always on in Advanced):
  - **Clothes** — Step-by-step: style → color → pattern, with visual swatches.
  - **Background** — Many categories and options; transparent background is available on paid tiers.
  - **Color grading** — Presets: B&W, Warm, Cool, Cinematic, Sepia, Pastel, High Contrast.
  - **Regional edit** — Limit changes to background, clothing, lighting, hair, or color grading only.
  - **Custom instruction** — Type what you want changed.
  - **Prompt history** — Reuse your last 15 instructions with one click.
- **Undo / Redo** — Full history with step counter.
- **Edit history strip** — Thumbnail timeline; click any step to go back.
- **Save to Library** — Save portraits to your library (limits depend on plan).
- **Feature tour** — Short guided tour for first-time users.

---

### 5. Export & Share

- **Aspect ratio** — 1:1 (square) or 3:4 (portrait).
- **Layout** — Fill (crop) or Fit (letterbox with blurred background).
- **Crop position** — Adjust position when using Fill.
- **Format** — JPG (free) or PNG (paid; needed for transparent background).
- **Platform presets** — One-click sizes for:
  - LinkedIn (800×800)
  - GitHub (500×500)
  - X / Twitter (400×400)
  - Instagram (320×320)
  - Resume / CV (600×800)
- **Download All Platforms** — One ZIP with all five preset sizes (Plus).
- **Share** — Share to LinkedIn or Twitter, or copy the app link.

---

### 6. Account & Security

- **Sign in** — Google or email/password.
- **Session timeout** — Automatic sign-out after 15 minutes of inactivity. A warning appears about 1 minute before. Timeout is paused during portrait generation.
- **Profile** — Update profile, preferences, account, and billing (e.g. open billing portal).
- **Delete account** — Remove your account and associated data from Profile → Account.

---

### 7. Saved Portraits Library

- Save favorite portraits to a personal library (limits: Free 10, Basic 50, Plus 200).
- Open the library from the app to view, reload into the editor, or delete saved portraits.

---

### 8. Admin (For Administrators)

Administrators have a dedicated area to:

- **User list** — Search and filter users (e.g. by plan and status).
- **Stats** — Total users, paid users, suspended, daily usage, cost.
- **User detail** — Profile, onboarding answers, usage (generations, edits, exports, saves), account timeline.
- **Subscription** — Change plan, suspend or unsuspend a user, delete a user and their data.

## Privacy (Summary)

- Photos are processed by AI to generate portraits.
- Session portraits are temporary; saved portraits are stored in your library according to your plan.
- The app collects account info (e.g. email, name), usage stats, and onboarding preferences to personalize the experience.
- You can delete your account and associated data from your profile at any time.
